Welcome to our page

Join us as we explore this fascinating (not to mention enjoyable) martial art that is focussed on the throw, pin, lock and fall.

About Us

The O-KO Judo Club has been providing judo classes in and around Sydney for over 2 years.

Prospective members and visitors who are experienced in Judo (or a similar martial art) are always welcome in our general training classes.

Our Specialization

Prospective members who are new to the sport of Judo are encouraged to take part in our Judo Starter courses.

What We do

Our training focuses on basic to intermediate level Judo skills and allows participants to progress with confidence into our regular training classes.
